What platforms does Metaplay support?

Metaplay offers games for a wide range of platforms, including PC, PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo Switch. We strive to have something for every gamer, no matter your preferred console or system.

How do I purchase a game from Metaplay?

Simply browse our store, select the game you want, and add it to your cart. Once you complete the checkout process, you’ll receive a digital code or download link, depending on the product.

Does Metaplay offer discounts or deals?

Yes! We regularly feature discounts, bundles, and exclusive deals on a variety of games. Be sure to check our "Deals" section and subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated. .

What are Metaplay Originals?

Metaplay Originals are our exclusive games developed in-house. These unique titles are only available through Metaplay and are crafted with love by our team of passionate developers .

What is your refund policy?

Refunds are available for eligible purchases under certain conditions. If you encounter issues with a game or product, please contact our customer support team for assistance.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via email, live chat on our website, or through the Contact Us page. We’re here to help with any questions or concerns!

Does Metaplay support indie developers?

Absolutely! We’re proud to support indie game creators by offering their games on our platform and providing resources to help them succeed. Check out our "Indie Spotlight" section for the latest indie titles.

Can I gift games to friends?

Yes, you can! Many of our games include a gifting option during checkout. Simply choose the “Gift” option, enter your friend’s email, and they’ll receive a digital code for the game.

Can I track my order?

For digital purchases, you’ll instantly receive your order in your account or email. If you’re purchasing physical merchandise (if applicable), tracking details will be shared once the item ships.
